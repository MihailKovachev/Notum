>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Open Set
>
>Let $(S, \tau)$ be a [topological space](Topological%20Space.md).
>
>A [subset](../Set%20Theory/Subset.md) of $S$ is an **open set** if it is a member of $\tau$.
>
