>
>$$l(t) = \vec{r} + t\vec{v}$$
>
>for some nonzero [vectors](../../../../Algebra/Linear%20Algebra/Matrices/Row%20&%20Column%20Vectors/Real%20Vectors/Real%20Vector.md) $\vec{r}, \vec{v} \in \mathbb{R}^n$.
